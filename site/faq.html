Frequently asked

Is this legal advice?

No. Compass is operational compliance guidance — like having a senior safety person on your team. For court cases, criminal exposure, or material litigation, we'll tell you to call a transportation attorney. Every regulatory answer cites 49 CFR so you can verify.

What if the AI is wrong?

Every answer cites the 49 CFR section so you can verify. We use Claude (Anthropic) — one of the most accurate AI models — grounded on our 100-skill corpus reviewed by Mike Perry, 20-year NY State Police CMV inspector. We carry E&O insurance for platform liability. Material decisions should still be confirmed with your safety officer or counsel.

Do I have to integrate my ELD / MVR / drug-test provider?

No, but it makes Compass much more useful. On the free + Starter tiers, you can do everything manually. Pro + Enterprise tiers include integrations with Samsara, Motive, Geotab, Quest, LabCorp, FMCSA Clearinghouse, Comdata, EFS, and more.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Self-serve cancel button. No friction, no exit interview, no save-call. We'd rather you come back when it's right than feel trapped.

What's the relationship between X3 Compass and X3 Fleet Safety?

X3 Fleet Safety LLC is the parent company. X3 Compass is the AI-native product line. Both serve small motor carriers; Compass is what most new customers buy. If you're currently a customer of the X3 Fleet Safety platform, you'll see Compass features added to your existing account at no cost.
