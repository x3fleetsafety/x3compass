WHAT'S INSIDE

Twelve hazmat brains. One subscription.

Add to any Compass tier. Auto-activates when your fleet logs its first hazmat load.

01 · PLACARDS

Placard Verifier

Driver photographs all 4 sides before departure. AI verifies: placard present, correct class for the load, ID number panel readable, condition acceptable. Fails the check → driver gets specific fix instructions before rolling.

02 · PAPERWORK

Shipping Paper Validator

Upload the BOL. AI checks every required element per 49 CFR 172.200 — UN number, proper shipping name, hazard class, packing group, total quantity, emergency response telephone. Missing field → flagged before driver leaves the dock.

03 · LOOKUP

ERG Instant Lookup

Type a UN number. Get the Emergency Response Guide page with isolation distances, protection distances, fire response, spill response, first aid. 3,500+ UN numbers. Driver-app friendly for roadside use.

04 · COMPATIBILITY

Segregation Checker

Add 2+ materials to the load. Instantly see if they're compatible per 49 CFR 177.848. Class 3 + Class 5.1 oxidizer = blocked with explanation. Saves the load from becoming a roadside violation.

05 · CREDENTIALS

H Endorsement + TSA Tracker

Every hazmat driver tracked: CDL H endorsement expiration, TSA Hazmat Threat Assessment renewal (5-year cycle), state-specific re-tests. Reminders at 90/60/30/14 days. Lapse → driver auto-removed from hazmat dispatch.

06 · TRAINING

HM-126 Training Manager

49 CFR 172.700 requires hazmat training every 3 years. Per driver: initial date, recurring date, certificate file, training topics. Auto-reminder 90 days before lapse. Audit-ready records.

07 · REGISTRATION

PHMSA Registration Tracker

Annual hazmat registration (July 1 – June 30 cycle). Compass tracks your tier, fee paid, certificate, renewal deadline. Auto-renew reminders. Certificate downloads to your driver app for in-vehicle compliance.

08 · TANKS

Cargo Tank Scheduler

Per tank: spec (DOT-406/407/412/MC-330/331), inspection cycle (annual visual, 1-5 year hydrostatic), certifications. Compass schedules tests, generates reminder, holds vehicle from dispatch if test lapsed.

09 · ROUTES

Hazmat Route Planner

Avoids tunnels (Holland, Lincoln, Big Dig), follows state-designated routes (CA, NY, NJ, IL), handles Class 7 highway-route-controlled radioactive. Driver app shows compliant turn-by-turn. State permits surfaced.

10 · SECURITY

Hazmat Security Plan

For carriers transporting security-sensitive hazmat (per 49 CFR 172.800): personnel security, unauthorized access prevention, en-route security, training. Annual review reminders. Compass-drafted template.

11 · INCIDENTS

Form 5800.1 Incident Assistant

Hazmat incident happens? Compass walks the driver through the immediate-notification call (1-800-424-8802), then drafts Form 5800.1 within the 30-day window. Auto-pulls load + driver + carrier details.

12 · DEFENSE

Hazmat DataQ Scanner

Hazmat violations have higher CSA weight + bigger fines. Compass scans every roadside hazmat inspection for contestable patterns — wrong placard code, improperly assigned subsidiary hazard, expired-but-actually-current cargo tank certs. Drafts DataQ challenges.

What goes wrong without it.

The hazmat violations that ruin a Friday — and how Compass catches them first.

Driver leaves dock with 3 placards instead of 4.
Level 1 inspection on I-80. Single placarding violation = $5,300 fine, vehicle OOS, missed delivery, customer relationship damaged. Compass: photo-verifies all 4 sides before driver leaves dock.

Roadside inspector finds the gap. $1,500-$8,000 violation. Carrier OOS until corrected. Compass: validates shipping paper before vehicle leaves shipper.

Segregation violation per 49 CFR 177.848. Inspector finds at scale. Fine + load returned for re-loading + cargo damage. Compass: blocks the load assignment before dispatch.

QUESTIONS

Common questions about Hazmat Center.

Do I need a hazmat endorsement to add Hazmat Center?

You don't — but your drivers do, if you transport placard-required hazmat. Hazmat Center actually helps you track which drivers have valid H endorsements + TSA Threat Assessments, so you don't dispatch them on hazmat loads when expired.

What if I only haul hazmat occasionally?

Hazmat Center auto-pauses if no hazmat loads are logged for 60 days. You're not charged when you're not using it. When you book a hazmat load again, it reactivates.

Is this legal advice?

No. Hazmat Center is operational compliance guidance — like having a senior hazmat compliance person on your team. For court / criminal / litigation, we'll tell you to call a transportation attorney. Every regulatory answer cites 49 CFR so you can verify.

How accurate is the AI?

Every answer is grounded on the 100-skill open-source hazmat corpus (Apache 2.0, public on GitHub). Every CFR citation is verifiable. We use Claude (Anthropic) — among the most accurate AI models — and the corpus is reviewed by senior FMCSA + PHMSA compliance veterans.

What about IATA (air) and IMDG (ocean) hazmat?

Hazmat Center includes IATA DGR and IMDG Code coverage. If you ship hazmat multimodally (truck to air to ocean), Compass handles the harmonization. Note: IATA-certified air-shipment personnel are still required by law for air; Compass supports but doesn't replace them.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Self-serve cancel. No friction. 30-day money-back guarantee on top.

What about Canada cross-border (TDG)?

TDG (Canada's hazmat regs) is covered. We handle the harmonization with US 49 CFR. Coming Q1: Cross-Border Center as a separate add-on for full Canada + Mexico operations.

Does this work for the smallest carriers (1-5 trucks)?

Yes. Hazmat Center is the same price ($99/mo) regardless of fleet size. A 1-truck owner-op hauling hazmat gets the same coverage as a 50-truck fleet.
