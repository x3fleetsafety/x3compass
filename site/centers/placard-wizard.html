STEP 2 OF 4 · QUANTITY

How much, and in what packaging?

Bulk shipments always require placards. Non-bulk depends on aggregate weight + material class.

Bulk Single packaging >119 gal liquid, 882 lb solid, or cargo tank trailer
Non-bulk Drums, IBCs, cylinders, multiple smaller packages

The 1,001 lb rule: non-bulk hazmat under 1,001 lbs (aggregate) generally doesn't require placards. Exceptions apply for high-hazard classes.
