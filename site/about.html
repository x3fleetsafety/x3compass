About X3 Compass.

X3 Compass is the AI Virtual Safety Director for small US motor carriers. We replace the $130K/year Safety Director with software that knows 49 CFR cold — for $79–$999/month.

Origin

X3 Fleet Safety LLC operated a DOT compliance SaaS for small carriers since 2024. In 2026, after publishing 100 open-source compliance skills (Apache 2.0, github.com/x3fleetsafety/skills), we realized the same content + Claude AI could replace a human Safety Director for the carriers who couldn't afford one. Compass was born.

Mission

Make compliance work for the 125,000+ small US motor carriers who don't have a full-time safety team — and the safety consultants who serve them. We do this by building the most regulatorily accurate AI in trucking + giving our compliance review team a megaphone.

Open source

The 100-skill DOT compliance corpus that powers Compass is published Apache 2.0 at github.com/x3fleetsafety/skills. The Compass platform code itself is at github.com/x3fleetsafety/x3compass — Apache 2.0. We believe AI compliance tools should be transparent, auditable, and contestable.
