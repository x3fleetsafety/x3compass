What Compass actually does

01

Answers regulatory questions

"When does the Clearinghouse Full Query have to be done?" Compass cites 49 CFR 382.701 and gives the operational steps. Every answer is sourced.

02

Watches your driver files

CDL expiring in 30 days. Medical card lapsing. Annual MVR overdue. Drug test failure. Compass surfaces them before they're problems.

03

Prepares you for audits

New-entrant audit in 60 days? Compass generates the punchlist, finds the gaps, and drafts the documents.

04

Coaches your drivers

Harsh-brake event on Tuesday? Compass drafts the coaching message you should send, with the right tone and CFR backing.

05

Drafts your safety meetings

Weekly safety meeting agenda. 52-topic library. Customized to events from your fleet.
