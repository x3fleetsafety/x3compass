PRODUCT

Compass is six brains in one.

Pick what you need. They all share the same fleet context, so the audit-prep brain knows what your driver-file brain knows.

💬
BRAIN 01

Q&A Compass

"When does the Clearinghouse Full Query have to be done?" Compass cites 49 CFR 382.701 and gives you the operational steps, in plain English, in under 3 seconds. Every answer sourced. Every CFR section linked.

Try asking: "Can my driver still drive if the medical card expired yesterday?"
📅
BRAIN 02

Calendar Compass

Every driver file is watched. CDL expiring in 30 days. Medical card in 14. Annual MVR due. UCR renewal coming. 2290 filing window. Compass surfaces them with the urgency they deserve and drafts the reminder messages for you.

You get: Email + SMS reminders at 30 / 14 / 7 / 1 day intervals, fully automated.
📋
BRAIN 03

Audit Compass

Got a new-entrant audit in 60 days? A compliance review notice? Compass scans your entire fleet, identifies every gap, drafts every missing document, and assembles a binder-ready punchlist sorted by what an auditor opens first.

Output: Word/PDF audit-prep package with every DQF gap flagged, every 396 record verified, every policy current.
🎯
BRAIN 04

Coaching Compass

Dashcam event Tuesday? Harsh-brake pattern? Hours-of-service edge? Compass drafts the coaching message you should send — with the right tone, the relevant data, and CFR backing — so you can review, sign, and send in 30 seconds.

You get: Draft message, tone-calibrated by the event severity + driver's recent history.
📢
BRAIN 05

Meeting Compass

Every Monday, Compass generates a 15–30-minute safety meeting agenda. Topic rotates across the 52-week curriculum. Customized to incidents from your fleet that week. Includes one driver Q&A prompt. Send via email or print.

Output: One-page PDF meeting agenda + email-to-drivers template + 5-minute video script.
⚖️
BRAIN 06

DataQ Compass

The highest-ROI compliance activity is contesting wrongly-assigned violations. Compass scans your inspection history, flags the disputable ones, drafts the DataQ challenge letter with the right CFR citation and evidence demand. You review and submit.

Win-rate uplift: Carriers using Compass DataQ flag ~30% win rate on disputes (vs ~15% national average for unflagged self-submitters).

HONEST ANSWERS

Everything you might be wondering.

Is this legal advice?

No. Compass is operational compliance guidance — like having a senior safety person on your team. For court cases, criminal exposure, or material litigation, we'll tell you to call a transportation attorney. Every regulatory answer cites 49 CFR so you can verify.

What if the AI is wrong?

Every answer cites the 49 CFR section so you can verify. We use Claude (Anthropic) — one of the most accurate AI models available — grounded on our 100-skill corpus reviewed by senior FMCSA compliance veterans. We carry E&O insurance for platform liability. Material decisions should still be confirmed with your safety officer or counsel.

Do I have to integrate my ELD?

No, but it makes Compass much more useful. On Free + Starter you do everything manually. Pro + Enterprise integrate with Samsara, Motive, Geotab, Quest, LabCorp, Clearinghouse, Comdata, EFS, and more.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Self-serve cancel button. No friction, no exit interview, no save-call. We'd rather you come back when it's right than feel trapped.

What's the relationship to X3 Fleet Safety?

X3 Fleet Safety LLC is the parent company. X3 Compass is the AI-native product line. Both serve small motor carriers; Compass is what most new customers buy. Existing X3 Fleet Safety customers see Compass features added at no cost.

How is my data secured?

TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest, row-level security at the database level. Your fleet data is isolated per tenant. We never sell or share driver/carrier data. Privacy policy here.

Who reviews the AI's regulatory accuracy?

Senior FMCSA compliance veterans audit the 100-skill corpus quarterly. The corpus is also public (Apache 2.0) — anyone can inspect it, file issues, or contribute corrections.
