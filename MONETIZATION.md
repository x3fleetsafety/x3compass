# X3 Compass — Monetization Framework

Seven revenue streams. Each compounds on the others.

## 1. Direct subscriptions (primary)
- Free / Starter ($79) / Pro ($299) / Enterprise ($999) monthly
- Annual plan: 15% discount (improves cash flow + retention)
- Multi-truck volume add-on: +$5/truck/mo above tier cap

## 2. Partner subscriptions (primary)
- Compass Partner: $499/mo flat, unlimited carrier seats, white-label
- Premium Partner: $999/mo, includes geographic territory exclusivity + Mike Perry 1:1 quarterly
- Master Partner: $1,999/mo, includes co-marketing budget + speaking opportunities

## 3. Add-on services (transactional)
- DataQ dispute filing: $99 per filing (we draft, customer reviews + submits)
- Audit defense package: $1,499 one-time (when carrier is selected for a compliance review)
- Annual compliance audit (Mike Perry signed): $499 one-time
- Custom safety policy generation: $299
- New-entrant safety audit prep: $499 one-time
- DOT registration / authority renewal service: $99
- Compliance health check (one-time deep scan): $199

## 4. Affiliate program (acquisition cost)
- 30% lifetime recurring on Direct customer subscriptions
- $200 one-time bounty on every approved Partner
- Net cost: ~25% of revenue from affiliate-attributed customers
- Earned media at scale, no front-loaded ad spend

## 5. Marketplace / integration revenue share
- **Insurance broker referrals:** when Compass identifies a carrier needing better coverage and refers to insurance partner — 10% commission
- **ELD vendor partnership:** Samsara/Motive/Geotab pay X3 a SaaS revenue share when carrier signs up through Compass referral (typical: 5–10%)
- **Background-check vendor:** Checkr Partner-tier already in place; pass-through margin + volume rebates
- **Truck financing:** carrier needs new tractors → Compass refers to OEM captive finance; ~$500–$2,000/truck commission
- **Truck insurance:** carrier insurance renewal triggers referral → 5–10% Year-1 commission

Estimated revenue at scale: **$2–5M/year** from marketplace alone.

## 6. Premium content (skills repo)
The 100-skill open-source corpus is the free funnel. Premium content goes deeper:
- **"Compass University"** — paid course tracks ($299–$999):
  - Safety Director Certification (40-hour curriculum)
  - DataQ Mastery (8-hour, $299)
  - New-Entrant Audit Prep (12-hour, $499)
  - Hazmat Compliance Bootcamp (24-hour, $799)
  - Partner Practice Builder (40-hour, $999, included with Partner tier)
- Sold through compass.x3fleetsafety.com/university
- Apache 2.0 base corpus stays free (preserves marketing flywheel)

## 7. Sponsorships + ads (low-volume, high-margin)
- **Skills repo sponsorship banner:** integration partners (Samsara, Motive, Quest, LabCorp) pay $5K–$15K/year to be the featured integration in relevant skills
- **Newsletter sponsorships:** $2K–$5K/issue when X3 newsletter reaches 10K+ subscribers
- **Mike Perry-fronted webinars sponsored by insurance brokers/OEMs:** $5K–$10K each
- **Conference speaking + sponsorships:** Mid-America Trucking Show, ATA Management Conference, NPTC — pay-for-presence model
- Total at scale: ~$300K/year
