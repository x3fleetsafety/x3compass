Known hazmat restrictions

NYC Metro
Holland Tunnel: ALL hazmat prohibited. Lincoln Tunnel: limited classes with permit. Queens-Midtown: hazmat prohibited. Use Goethals/Outerbridge.
Boston
I-90/I-93 Big Dig tunnels: hazmat prohibited. Sumner, Callahan, Ted Williams: prohibited. Required routing via I-95 around Boston.
SF Bay Area
Caldecott Tunnel: hazmat prohibited. Bay Bridge Yerba Buena tunnel: prohibited. Routing via San Mateo Bridge / Dumbarton.
