Four required training elements (49 CFR 172.704)

ELEMENT 1
General awareness / familiarization
Hazmat regulations overview, HMT structure, hazard recognition. All employees.
ELEMENT 2
Function-specific
Job-specific training. Loader, dispatcher, driver — each gets their function's content.
ELEMENT 3
Safety
Emergency response, PPE, accident prevention.
ELEMENT 4
Security awareness
Post-9/11 addition. Threats, suspicious activity, security plan basics.
